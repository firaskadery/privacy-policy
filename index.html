Privacy Policy for Rima Game

Last updated: July 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our mobile application "Rima Game" (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

When you use our App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Game Data: Your game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Device Information: Our advertising partners may collect device information for ad personalization
  • Purchase Information: Information about in-app purchases is processed by Google Play Store
  • Usage Analytics: Basic app usage statistics to improve our game

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the game functionality
  • To save your game progress and settings
  • To process in-app purchases through Google Play
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To improve and optimize the game experience
  • To provide customer support when needed

3. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. View their Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases. View their Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services: For app functionality and analytics

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences
  • We do not store personal information on our servers
  • Purchase information is securely handled by Google Play Store
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • To our trusted third-party service providers (Google AdMob, Google Play) who assist us in operating our app
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Delete the app and all locally stored data at any time
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Request information about your data (though we store minimal data)
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

8. Data Retention

  • Game data is retained locally on your device until you delete the app
  • Advertising data is handled according to Google AdMob's retention policies
  • Purchase records are maintained by Google Play according to their policies

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable privacy laws and provide adequate protection for your personal information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are from the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for collecting and using the personal information depends on the context:

  • Performance of a contract (providing game services)
  • Legitimate interests (improving our app, advertising)
  • Your consent (where explicitly given)
